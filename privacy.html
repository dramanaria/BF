Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who We Are

DRAMAN® Foundation is a charitable organisation registered in Kyiv, Ukraine. We operate the website draman.space and related subdomains. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at info@draman.space.

Information We Collect

We may collect personally identifiable information — such as your name, email address, and phone number — when you contact us directly, register for our programmes, or subscribe to DRAMAN Art Club.

We also collect non-personal information automatically, such as browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site, through standard web analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve our services, communicate with you about our programmes and events, process registrations, and respond to your inquiries. We do not use your data for commercial purposes.

Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our content. Google Analytics collects anonymised data about your visit. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the site.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to provide services you have requested.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable law.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@draman.space.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
